First-run setup: which approach feels right?

When the dashboard loads with no settings, how should it prompt you to configure your industry and states?

A

Full-screen setup wizard

Dashboard is blocked until you choose industry + states. Step 1: pick your industry. Step 2: pick your states. Then the dashboard loads. Clean, no distractions — you can't accidentally skip it.

Pros

  • Can't be missed
  • Focused decision

Cons

  • Feels heavyweight
  • Delays seeing the dashboard
B

Centered modal overlay

Dashboard renders behind a modal dialog: "Welcome — tell us your industry and states to personalize your briefing." Both selectors on one screen. Click Save to dismiss and load. Can be dismissed with a default (Banking / CA+NV).

Pros

  • Lightweight feel
  • One step, then go

Cons

  • Easy to dismiss without reading
C

Inline banner + auto-open settings

Dashboard loads normally, but the ⚙ Settings drawer opens automatically and a banner at the top says "Set up your profile to personalise this briefing." Settings close when you save. No modal, no wizard.

Pros

  • Minimal interruption
  • See the dashboard immediately

Cons

  • Easy to ignore the drawer
